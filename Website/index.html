Data Security

We understand the sensitivity and confidentiality of
Healthcare data. All your data is 256 bit encrypted, the
same level as banks encrypt the financial transactions
and are stored in HIPPA compliant cloud servers. Your
data remains accessible only to you and your team.

Customization

You can customise the platform to suit your clinical
requirements. Starting from assessment templates/
questionnaire to the appointment data parameters,
you remain in-charge and can update it at anytime
you want. This makes our platform unique to support
your individual practice requirement.

Practice management

Effortless scheduling, accounding, invoice and
billing, history, reminders, etc helps in automating
all your daily operations. AeglePro helps you and
your team to be more productive in treating the
patients and worry less about the paperwork. You
remain fully in control of what goes on in your
practice from anywhere, anytime.

Access Control

You can define the level of access to your
team members so that not all the data is
accessible for every member of your team.
Control the access to individual modules as
well as the functionalities within them.

Custom exercise library to prescribe exercises

Clinic Website for Patients

Get a dedicated website for your clinic where your
patients can view their details. You can also link this to
your existing clinic website if you have one for
appointment requests & patient registration.
